This algorithmic behavior forms a wonderful resonance with the proposition of "being born and dying simultaneously" in Zhuangzi's "On the Equality of Things" - each particle is both a deterministic three-dimensional coordinate point and the amplitude of a wave function in Hilbert space.

Dimensional expansion of Peng: 

The "Peng"-shaped dimension-raising movement guided by the left hand corresponds to the equipotential surface climb of particle groups in the four-dimensional Perlin noise field. This is not only a mathematical metaphor of "When the sea is in motion, it will migrate to the southern ocean" in "A Happy Excursion", but also a visual interpretation of the concept of connection in differential geometry: the audience's gesture is actually selecting a gauge field in the fiber bundle and guiding the particles to complete the dimensional transition along the parallel movement path.

- Projection of Lorentzian manifolds with conformal compactification:
The color flow in the background is actually a mapping of a conformally compactified Lorentzian manifold on a two-dimensional screen. The Lorentzian manifold is a mathematical object that describes the structure of spacetime, while conformal compactification "converges" the infinitely extended spacetime into a finite range, enabling the infinite distance to be represented in a finite way. This mapping allows us to present an infinitely extended and never-ending dynamic flow effect in two-dimensional space.

- Visual paradox of eternal fall:
This effect of continuous falling has a wonderful correspondence with the eternal falling solution in general relativity. In some strong gravitational fields (such as near black holes), objects will continuously fall but the moment when they truly reach the end point is never seen. Here, the color flow symbolizes this dynamic process - they maintain a certain dynamic balance while continuously falling, always approaching but never actually "landing".

### Touchdesigner Kunpeng construction
We imported the model into TouchDesigner (TD). First, we used the mouse to create a simple interactive test for controlling the position of Kunpeng. And we connected the real-time image output of Kunpeng to ComfyUI.

For the choice of visual changes, we chose the dynamics and blending of particles. Because this is similar to the feedback loop in a topological network. In topology, a continuous feedback loop can be regarded as a mapping that continuously pulls back the system state and promotes its evolution to a new state. Similarly, our visual system intuitively presents the iterative process of the algorithm by rendering the dynamic changes of particles in real time. Each gathering and dispersing change of particles is not only an instantaneous manifestation of the local state but also an externalized manifestation of the feedback mechanism of the entire system.

Just as emphasized in our project concept: the continuous transformation and mutual integration between all things.

### Touchdesigner gesture interaction construction
We later added a webcam and made the interaction method into two-handed control and conducted tests.

The two hands each undertake different control tasks, just like the dual identities of Kun and Peng in ancient mythology. The right hand triggers the collapse and aggregation of the particle system, presenting a local minimum state in chaos (that is, the form of "Kun"), while the left hand guides the particles to expand and rise in a higher dimension, symbolizing the transformation from chaos to an orderly and grand "Peng". This division of labor makes the interactive experience more layered and reflects the dynamic balance and mutual transformation between the two states.

## Design Justification 
- Interaction design improvement:
  
Issue: The two-handed interaction method is not intuitive and has a high learning cost.

Intuitive feedback: Design clear visual feedback for the user's gesture operations, such as displaying the current transformation status in real time on the screen (such as using a progress bar, color gradient, or icon prompts), so that users can clearly see the correspondence between gestures and effects at a glance.

- Internationalization reconstruction:
  
Issue: The mythical image of Kunpeng lacks a cognitive basis for non-East Asian audiences.
