**Project Description** 

The ongoing project involves a game centered around digital footprints and their ethical ramifications. It features depictions of keyboard interactions and a lively robot with particle effects to highlight how our online behaviors leave lasting marks in the virtual realm. Players engage with the game by guiding the robot's movements using keyboard commands. Each keypress traces the robot's journey, in a representation of the digital footprints individuals create. These imprints then. Propagate within the vast web of data networks. The robot releases particles from its head whenever a key is pressed, symbolizing the transmission of personal information with the aim of sparking discussions and raising awareness about data privacy through this interactive simulation. The core concept of digital footprints is embodied here, but behind this design, is it unintentionally transforming human digital behavior into algorithm-driven graphics, making people's actions more mechanized and predictable?

I developed this project with p5js. The WEBGL component establishes a setting, on the screen. Functions such as createCanvas() and loadModel() are used to load the keyboard and 3D head models to simulate keyboard input and robot actions, with particle effects included in the process.The keyPressed() function is responsible for detecting keystrokes and adjusting the robot's position along with the particle system.Custom class Particle and an array named robotPath are utilized to control the movement of the robot and particles, for user interaction. 

The project began by planning out the layout of the keyboard meticulously using math formulas and loops to ensure each key was evenly distributed on the screen canvas. After that, I worked on creating the designs and arranging the particle system setup process, starting with loading the head model. Then use the createParticles() function to generate particles. Users controlled the movement of the robot, with each keystroke leaving a trail behind to help them keep track of their progress. This method enhanced the user experience by incorporating captivating particle effects throughout. I've sometimes relied on ChatGPT for help, with code syntax and finding resources in projects to assist me on my journey. During my process, I found inspiration in art and digital privacy principles as I worked to blend data visualization methods, with user privacy concerns.

The idea for this concept originated during a conversation, with a friend on WeChat, about purchasing ski equipment a memory surfaced of a skiing experience. After chatting, I noticed an increase in equipment advertisements appearing online which made me understand the significance of data analytics. 
Protecting our privacy is a project that goes beyond artistry—it also acts as a mirror of the ongoing struggles concerning digital privacy in today's society. It resonates with my commitment to ensuring data protection and advocating for transparency and fairness in algorithms. I trust that users will gain insight into their presence and contemplate ethical considerations related to privacy by engaging with this project. If I can , I will add privacy functionalities and visual representations of data in this project.
